Then, a little salt and sugar should be added and placed in a container. It should be covered and left for a few days to sour. To make apple preserve, apples need to be cut into small pieces. Then, they should be cooked in syrup. The flavor can be enhanced by adding cardamom, cinnamon, and clove powder to the syrup. To make apple chutney, peel the apples, remove the seeds, and grind them finely. Then, mix in salt, chili, ginger, mint, coriander, and lemon juice thoroughly. To make apple pie, cut apples into small pieces and mix them with sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, and butter. Then, place them in a dish and cover with a dough crust. After that, it should be baked in an oven. To make apple kheer, peel and grate the apples. Then, cook them in milk. Afterward, add sugar, almonds, raisins, and cardamom.
